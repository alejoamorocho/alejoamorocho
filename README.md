### 🦋 About Me

I'm a **Product Engineer** who sees the world through the lens of the **butterfly effect** — small, intentional changes that cascade into massive outcomes. I'm fascinated by how **order emerges from chaos**, and I bring that same mindset to everything I build.

I work across the full stack with **TypeScript**, **Go**, and **Python** on **GCP/Firebase**, turning complex problems into products that amplify real objectives. Not just features that exist — tools that *change things*.

When I'm not shipping code, you'll find me lost in a book 📚, fueling up on coffee ☕, or figuring out how to help someone with whatever they need 🤝

> *"Does the flap of a butterfly's wings in Brazil set off a tornado in Texas?"*
> — Edward Lorenz... and also kind of my engineering philosophy.
