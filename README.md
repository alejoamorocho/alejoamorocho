<h3 align="center">🦋 About Me</h3>

<p align="center">
I'm a <b>Product Engineer</b> who sees the world through the lens of the <b>butterfly effect</b> — small, intentional changes that cascade into massive outcomes. I'm fascinated by how <b>order emerges from chaos</b>, and I bring that same mindset to everything I build.
<br/><br/>
I work across the full stack with <b>TypeScript</b>, <b>Go</b>, and <b>Python</b> on <b>GCP/Firebase</b>, turning complex problems into products that amplify real objectives. Not just features that exist — tools that <i>change things</i>.
<br/><br/>
When I'm not shipping code, you'll find me lost in a book 📚, fueling up on coffee ☕, or figuring out how to help someone with whatever they need 🤝
